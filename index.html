History

General Relativity was formulated by Albert Einstein in 1915, revolutionizing our understanding of gravity. Instead of being a force, gravity is the result of the curvature of spacetime caused by mass and energy.

Reformations

Over the years, General Relativity has been extended and reinterpreted in many ways, including black hole theories, gravitational waves, and quantum gravity approaches.
